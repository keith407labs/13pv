Bernoulli Distribution

The simplest discrete distribution — a single trial with two outcomes. Success (1) with probability p, failure (0) with probability 1 − p. The building block of binomial, geometric, and negative binomial models.

Probability Mass Function — P(X = x)

Probability Mass Function

P(X = x) = px(1 − p)1−x   for x ∈ {0, 1}
E[X] = p · Var(X) = p(1 − p)
