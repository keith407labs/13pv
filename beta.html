Beta Distribution

The Beta distribution is defined on [0, 1] and models proportions, probabilities, and rates. Its two shape parameters α and β create an extraordinary range of forms — from uniform to U-shaped to skewed to bell-curved.

Probability Density Function — f(x; α, β)

Density Function

f(x; α, β) = xα−1(1−x)β−1 / B(α, β)
B(α, β) = Γ(α)Γ(β) / Γ(α+β) · 0 ≤ x ≤ 1
